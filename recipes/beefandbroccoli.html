Beef and Broccoli

Description

An American-Chinese classic

Ingredients

  • 220g Bavette steak
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Spring Onions
  • One broccoli
  • Sauce ingredients
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Marinade the beef
  2. Mix the sauce
  3. Fry the beef
  4. Cook the rice
  5. Boil the broccoli for one minute, then leave to air
  6. Add beef and broccoli back to pan
  7. Add ginger, garlic and spring onions
  8. Add sauce
